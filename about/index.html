Celebrating the golden age of 8-bit chip music made using programmable sound generator sound chips or synthesizers in vintage arcade machines, computers and video game consoles.

Alex Mbugua Ngugi

Welcome to my chip music player! I'm Alex, a passionate developer and musician who grew up during the golden age of 8-bit and 16-bit gaming. This project combines my love for nostalgic chip tune music with modern web technologies.

The music featured here represents years of experimentation with synthesizers, audio programming, and modern production techniques. Each track is crafted to evoke the spirit of classic arcade games, home computers, and the demoscene culture, delivered in high-quality MP3 format.

Features

High-Quality MP3 Audio

All tracks are delivered in high-quality MP3 format, ensuring compatibility across all devices and browsers while maintaining excellent audio fidelity.

Mobile-First Design

Responsive design optimized for mobile devices with touch-friendly controls and seamless playback across all screen sizes.

Advanced Search

Powerful search functionality with live filtering by track title, album, genre, and format. Keyboard shortcuts for power users.

Custom Downloads

Create custom ZIP packages with your favorite tracks for offline listening.
